ZindeAI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

Introduction

ZindeAI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ZindeAI mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture (if you use Google Sign-In)
  • Health Goals: Dietary preferences, health objectives, and nutritional targets
  • Food Logs: Images of food, nutritional data, and meal history

Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: Approximate location (when you grant permission) for local recommendations
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, app interactions, crash reports

Camera and Photo Access

  • We request camera access to scan food items and barcodes
  • Images are processed using AI to identify food and calculate nutritional information
  • You can choose whether to save images to your account

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing AI-powered nutritional analysis
  • Tracking your dietary intake and health goals
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Improving app functionality and performance
  • Sending important updates and notifications
  • Analyzing app usage to enhance our service

Data Storage

  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase services to store your data securely
    • Firebase Authentication for user accounts
    • Cloud Firestore for user data and meal logs
    • Firebase Storage for food images (optional)
    • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting
  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data is stored on secure servers provided by Google Cloud

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, storage, analytics, and crash reporting
  • Google ML Kit: On-device AI for barcode scanning
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • Google AI (Gemini): Cloud-based AI for food recognition and nutritional analysis

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and location tracking

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Or contact us at: serdestgun@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained longer for statistical analysis

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication with Firebase
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using our Service, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for significant changes)

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
